Features

Everything for the shoot. Nothing you don't need.

A focused toolkit designed around one idea: get to a finished cut faster by thinking in shots.

Shot-by-shot recording

Each take becomes a scene. A live timeline at the bottom shows clip durations and your running total in real time.

Controlled durations

Set a max per clip (10 s – 2 min) and per film (30 s – 10 min). Recording stops automatically when the limit hits.

End-of-clip countdown

A 3 – 2 – 1 overlay appears 3 seconds before auto-stop, giving you time to land your movement cleanly.

Ghost overlay

The last frame of the previous clip overlays your preview — adjustable opacity and blend mode — so your continuity stays tight.

Locked orientation

Set portrait or landscape on the first shot. The app locks it for the entire film so your format never drifts.

Zoom presets + pinch

0.5× / 1× / 3× quick-select (hardware permitting) plus continuous pinch. Resets to 1× between clips for a neutral start.

One-tap undo

Not happy with the last shot? Delete it in one tap and go again — no digging through menus.

Export to Photos

All clips merge into one finished video. Choose codec (HEVC / H.264 / auto) and quality before exporting.

Auto-resume

Clips save to disk as you go. Close the app, come back later — your project is exactly where you left it.

How it works

Three steps to a finished film.

No editing, no timeline scrubbing. Just shoot, and go.

1

Set your limits

Choose a maximum clip length and a maximum film length. TourneMonte keeps you within them automatically — no willpower required.

2

Record shot by shot

Hit record for each scene. The ghost overlay helps you nail transitions, the countdown tells you when a clip is ending, and one tap undoes any take you don't like.

3

Export your film

When you're done, TourneMonte joins every clip into a single video and saves it straight to your Photos library.
