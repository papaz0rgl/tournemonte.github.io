No data collection

Tournemonte does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device and has no server of any kind.

What this app does not do

  • Collect or share personal information
  • Send any data to third parties
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Access your contacts, location, or Apple ID
  • Require an account or login

Data stored on your device

Any video projects you create with Tournemonte remain exclusively on your device. You have full control over them and can delete them at any time.

Media access

Tournemonte may request access to your camera, microphone, and photo library in order to function. This data never leaves your device and is never shared with anyone.
